The Brave Abhi

15 MARCH 2019, FIRDAY
08:00PM, MUMBAI
-Salman Shaikh

Do you know who is ‘THE BRAVE -ABHI’? Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman is ‘THE BRAVE - ABHI’. He is an Indian Air Force (IAF) pilot. He was born on 21 June,1983. He is from Chennai in Tamil Nadu. He has 16 years’ experience in the Indian Air Force.

On 14 February 2019, there happened a terrible attack on CRPF (Central Reserve Force) soldiers in Pulwama attack. The Jaish-e-Mohammed (JeM) organization told to social media that they were responsible for a suicide attack on a CRPF Convoy in Kashmir, killing 40 soldiers. The Indian Security force planned to stop JeM organization because is the biggest terrorist group and he is giving training to attack India. In this plan including Prime Minister Narendra Modi, and the security force had promised that the IAF would give a proper reply to the Pulwama attack. The name of mission was ‘Air strikes in J-e-M biggest training camp near Balakot in Pakistan’.

The IAF did strike in Pakistan on 26 February 2019 at 3:30 am in JeM biggest training camp near Balakot. The IAF destroyed JeM camp but JeM did not died in these strikes. Then Pakistan Air Force (PAF) action on IAF. PAF followed Abhinandan with PAF, F-16. Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman, shot down PAF, F-16 and later even his jet crashed and the fell down in Pakistan 6 Km away from India.

Pakistan arrested IAF Commander Abhinandan Varthaman on Wednesday 27 February 2019. It has been said Wing Commander Abhinandan was not physically tortured by the Pakistani authorities, he went through a “lot of mental harassment” in Pakistan captivity. Then Indian demanded to Pakistan “safe and immediate return of the IAF pilot” They also said Pakistan, “well-advised to ensure that no harm comes to him”.

Later, Wing Commander Abhinandan was respectfully handed over to India by the Pakistani authorities. After 60 hours released from Pakistan. On 1 March ,2019 at 9:25 pm Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman returned to India. After he came to India, then he met his family and “Everybody is proud of Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman”.
